New Mutawalli Appointed by Wakf Board

Published on August 1, 2025

We are honored to announce the official appointment of Mohammed Mushtaque Ahmed as the new Mutawalli of the Masjid e Mehtab Ali Shah complex. The appointment was formally approved by the Maharashtra State Waqf Board following a series of consultations.

A Mutawalli is the official caretaker or trustee of a Waqf (an Islamic endowment of property), responsible for its administration and for ensuring that the income is used for the intended charitable or religious purposes. This is a significant hereditary role that carries great responsibility for the spiritual and communal well-being of the institution.

Mr. Ahmed brings a deep commitment to preserving the rich heritage of our complex and has pledged to work closely with the community and government officials. His primary focus will be on addressing the long-standing issue of illegal encroachment on the Masjid's endowed land.

"I am humbled by this responsibility and am dedicated to upholding the values and traditions of this sacred institution for future generations," said Mr. Ahmed.

The community has expressed strong support for his appointment and is optimistic about the future of the complex under his leadership. His vision includes not only protecting the Waqf property but also enhancing the community services we offer. We look forward to a new chapter of growth, preservation, and community service.
