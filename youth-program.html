Youth Islamic Studies Program

Nurturing the next generation with knowledge and faith.

About the Program

We are excited to launch our new weekend Islamic Studies Program designed to build a strong foundation in Islamic knowledge and values for the youth of our community.

Our engaging curriculum covers essential topics in a way that is both educational and inspiring for young minds. Classes are held in a supportive and welcoming environment where students are encouraged to ask questions, engage in discussions, and connect with their faith on a deeper level.

  • Curriculum: Quranic studies, stories of the Prophets, Islamic history, and essential prayers.
  • Age Group: 7 - 14 years.
  • Schedule: Saturday & Sunday mornings.
  • Location: Community Hall, Masjid e Mehtab Ali Shah Complex.

Why Enroll Your Child?
