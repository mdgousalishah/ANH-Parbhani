A Living History, A Thriving Community

For generations, the **Ashoorkhana Nale Hyder & Masjid Mehtab Ali Shah** complex has been a cornerstone of faith in Parbhani.

Registered as a historic Waqf property (MSBW/PBN/47/2010), our mission is to preserve the profound spiritual traditions and Nizam-era architecture of this sacred place. We are committed to serving the needs of our modern community while honoring the legacy of those who came before us.

An Unbroken Chain of Stewardship

Our leadership is rooted in a deep, documented history of service.

This historic document, the Silsila (Chain of Succession), details the lineage of caretakers who have preserved this sacred trust for generations. It is a testament to the unbroken commitment to faith and community, linking the original founders like Syed Mehtab Ali Shah to the present day. We are honored to continue this profound legacy.
