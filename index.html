Welcome to Our Historic Complex

For generations, Masjid e Mehtab Ali Shah and the surrounding Ashoorkhana Nale Hyder have served as a cornerstone of the community in Parbhani. It is a place of prayer, reflection, and gathering, holding deep spiritual and historical significance.

Our mission is to preserve the traditions, architecture, and values of this sacred place, while continuing to serve the needs of our community. We invite you to explore its history and join us in our activities.

Historic Landmarks

Explore the architectural gems and sacred grounds within our complex.

Ashoorkhana Nale Hyder

A sacred space for mourning and remembrance, central to our community's spiritual life and traditions.

Naqqar Khana

This historic drum house once announced important events and prayer times, echoing the traditions of the Nizam era.

Baradari

A twelve-doored pavilion designed for ventilation, serving as a timeless spot for community gatherings.

Graveyard

A peaceful and final resting place, reminding us of our heritage and the generations that came before us.
